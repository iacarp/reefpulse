🪸 ReefPulse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

ReefPulse stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. Your aquarium parameters, livestock lists, equipment data, and preferences remain entirely on your phone or browser.

2. What Data is Stored Locally

  • Water parameter test results (dates, values)
  • Your livestock inventory (fish, corals, invertebrates)
  • Equipment and maintenance logs
  • App preferences (language, reminders)
  • Wishlist and compatibility data

3. Notifications

If you enable push notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

ReefPulse does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar tools.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your data to third parties. Period.

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's storage/cache in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

7. Children's Privacy

ReefPulse does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for reef aquarium hobbyists of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact
